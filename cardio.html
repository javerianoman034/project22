What Is Cardio?

Cardio, or cardiovascular exercise, is any physical activity that elevates your heart rate and breathing for a sustained period to benefit your heart and blood vessels. It's also called aerobic exercise and includes activities like running, swimming, cycling, and dancing. Regular cardio strengthens the heart muscle, improves lung capacity, increases endurance, and can help with weight management and stress reduction.


Benefits Of Cardio

Strengthens your heart: Your heart becomes a more efficient pump, requiring less effort to circulate blood throughout your body.
Improves circulation: It enhances the function of your blood vessels.
Increases oxygen use: Your body uses oxygen more effectively to produce energy.
Boosts metabolism: It helps burn calories and can aid in weight management.
Reduces stress: Physical activity is known to have a positive impact on mental health.
