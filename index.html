Zumba

Zumba is a high-energy, dance-fitness program that blends Latin and international music with aerobic exercise to create a fun, party-like workout. It combines simple dance moves, such as salsa, merengue, and hip-hop, with routines that help improve cardiovascular fitness, muscle conditioning, balance, and flexibility. Because it's designed to be enjoyable, many people refer to it as "exercise in disguise".

Cardio

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, is any physical activity that raises your heart rate and breathing for a sustained period, strengthening your heart and lungs. Examples include running, swimming, cycling, dancing, and brisk walking. This type of exercise, also known as aerobic exercise, helps your body use oxygen more efficiently.

Yoga

Yoga is a mind-body practice that connects physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation to improve overall health and well-being. Derived from the Sanskrit word "yuj," meaning "to unite," it aims to create harmony between the mind and body. Today, yoga is practiced for a variety of reasons, from physical fitness and stress reduction to its original spiritual discipline.
