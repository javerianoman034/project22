What is Yoga?

Yoga is a mind-body practice that unites the body, mind, and spirit through physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. Derived from the Sanskrit word "yuj" (to unite), it originated in ancient India as a spiritual discipline to achieve harmony between mind and body and is practiced today for its physical, mental, and emotional benefits, such as improved flexibility, strength, and stress reduction


Benefits of Yoga

Yoga offers numerous physical and mental benefits, including improved flexibility, strength, balance, and posture. It also reduces stress, promotes mental clarity, and can support cardiovascular health by lowering blood pressure and heart rate. The practice can also help with pain management, improved sleep, and overall well-being.
